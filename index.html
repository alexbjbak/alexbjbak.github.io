Bio

Alex Bak is an anesthesiology resident at University of Toronto. He grew up splitting his time between Seoul and the countryside of South Korea and later in Southern Ontario. In medical school, he pursued advanced training in biomedical engineering. Alex has published in several peer-reviewed journals and has been funded by Institute of Medical Science and University of Toronto Faculty of Medicine. His current interests are in improving perioperative outcomes.
